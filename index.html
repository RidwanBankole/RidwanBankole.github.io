About Me

Bankole Ridwan A. is currently a data science intern at the ExploreAI Academy. He has worked on a diverse of data science/ML projects, from marketing to human resources management, health, and natural language processing (NLP). Of his previous projects, Ridwan is proud of his role in LLM for marketing projects. He and his team leverage the generative power of the Large Language Model (LLM) to align marketing content against user persona for better engagement and conversion. The end products helped the marketing team to generate persona-specific content in no time, with over a 30% increase in conversion rate.

How did Ridwan find himself in data science space? Ridwan is a licensed pharmacist. His curiosity in data science started when he learned how machine learning has revolutionized the drug discovery process by shortening the drug discovery pipeline. In 2020, he started pursuing a skill in data science and machine learning in line with his glowing passion for using ML and AI to contribute to healthcare and businesses.

Ridwan stands out by his solid commitment to learning about technology and using the knowledge to shape the future. His keen attention to detail, hard work, and commitment to achieving audacious goals made him graduate in the top 5% of the ALX data analytics program and was honored as a Gold Fellow of The Room.

With proficiency in statistical modeling, Power BI, SQL, Python, NLP, AWS architecture, and various machine learning and deep learning frameworks, Ridwan seeks to architect machine learning and artificial intelligence solutions that improve life and business.
