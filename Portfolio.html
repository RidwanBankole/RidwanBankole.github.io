In this project, I delved into a comprehensive clinical trial dataset comparing the efficacy of Auralin and Novodra, two oral insulin treatments. Through meticulous exploration and visualization, I uncovered patterns in dose-response relationships, identified the correlation between dose and side effects, and statistically compared the effectiveness of Auralin against Novodra in reducing hba1c levels.
